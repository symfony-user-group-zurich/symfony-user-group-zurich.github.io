User Group Info

  • 1 min read

The Symfony User Group Zürich is a community-driven organization that brings together developers and enthusiasts interested in the Symfony PHP framework.

The group provides a platform for members to share experiences, discuss best practices, and explore the latest developments in Symfony and related web technologies.

Regular meetups feature presentations from both local and international speakers, fostering knowledge exchange and networking opportunities.
